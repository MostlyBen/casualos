openLab Exchange Roles & Responsibilities


Problem Owner
* Request a problem from an openLab and review produced Versions that iterate towards a potential solution to the requested problem. 


Coach
* Communicate and coordinate with Problem Owners who select their openLab to understand their requested problem and assign Collaber(s) to work on producing Versions that iterate towards a potential solution to a Problem Owner’s requested problem. 
* Stay up-to-date on activity within and oversee an openLab (a digital, asynchronous community of Collabers and other stakeholders) and especially all Version development performed by a Collaber assigned to an openLab managed by the Coach. 
* Answer Collaber, Problem Owner, or other stakeholder questions, address miscommunication issues, and generally proactively ensure a positive openLab community and excellent Version outcomes. 
Collaber
* Receive assigned problems from Coach(s) and work on producing Versions that iterate towards a potential solution to a Problem Owner’s requested problem.
